About BostonHacks

BostonHacks is Boston University's annual hackathon. For 24 hours, you’ll come to BU to learn and create. You’ll forms teams to build a unique project around a problem or idea, learning about different technologies along the way.

This year we have tracks, paths which you can optionally follow which include a comprehensive set of workshops, mentors, and materials to interact with, which will help you along the way of creating an awesome hack.
